About Us

Who we are?

Getting a bus or airline ticket has never been a hassle-free task in Pakistan. Whether you had to drive to a bus terminal, go to a travel agent to book your ticket in advance or stand in long queues, it was always time-consuming. Until we stepped up with the idea to digitize ticket bookings for the country. Bookme, through its e-ticketing system has made these processes quick and easy. Our user-friendly application and website allow customers to browse through all available options and book their tickets in a few simple clicks. Bookme is Pakistan's leading e-ticketing platform and is facilitating a massive customer base every day. Apart from bus and air tickets, our platform allows users to book movie tickets, hotels, car rentals, train tickets, event passes, tours, and even vacation packages. No more long queues. Just download the mobile application or visit the website for fast booking and leverage our exclusive discounts.

Vision

To ensure a transparent, easy, and efficient e-ticketing system in Pakistan.
